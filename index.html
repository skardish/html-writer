You're an ancient Egyptian God competing against the other gods to create the perfect landscape for your subjugated peoples. You and the gods adjust the landscape to best suit your people, building farms, rivers, citites, villages-and places of worship. The god with the best-mostloved-landscape wins and claims the whole land.

Super Epic Title Layer is a title-placement game where players draw random title and place the piece to continue the beautiful landscape of the Nile and Nile delta. Each title must seamlessly continue the landscape while getting victory points by claimig important pieces of countryside and locations.
